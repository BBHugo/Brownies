Ingredients/Supplies

  • 3x Eggs
  • 1x Waffle
  • 3 Cucumbers (Dice 2, keep 1 whole)
  • 3 6-Sided dies
  • A will to live

Recipe

  1. Take your will to live, throw it in the trash
  2. When it's inn the trash, take 2 die and roll them. If you get 5 or more, destroy the last cucumber, 6 or less return your will to live
  3. If you destroyed the cucumber, next scarf the waffle
  4. Hopefully you've thrown up, if not then start over. If you have, scarf the 3 eggs then die
  5. Take yourself out of the oven... Oh yeah, you should've put yourself in the oven for 5 minutes, but now take yourself out.
  6. done :)
